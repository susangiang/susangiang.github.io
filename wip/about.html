Hi, I’m Susan!

I’m an INFP product designer with a passion for crafting experiences that impact people in meaningful ways. A generalist at heart, I studied Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I learned to approach problems hollistically and was unexpectedly exposed to the world of product design. I haven’t looked back since and continue to be motivated by all the good that we can do through empathy, technology, and a great design jam.

Outside of work, I enjoy all things creative, from illustrating for my side hustle, HeySoosie, to getting my hands dirty in clay making pottery and designing the (soon-to-be 5 star) island of my dreams on Animal Crossing New Horizons.
