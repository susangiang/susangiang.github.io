Symbility Intersect

Lessons learned from working at a design agency

The Story

This past summer, I was a Product Design intern at Symbility Intersect.

Symbility Intersect is an agency specializing in mobile and IoT product strategy, design thinking, and engineering excellence. It delivers the tools, agile process, and product management expertise to clients looking to build with the speed of startups. Their clients include Canada's largest companies including Northbridge Insurance, CIBC, TD Bank, LCBO, Indigo, Mastercard Canada, and more.

Throughout my 4-month internship, I was given the opportunity to take ownership over multiple projects, from designing end-to-end experiences of major platform features, creating illustrations and icons, to independently assembling a developer-ready, AODA-compliant styleguide.

While I cannot share the work I did, the following are some lessons learned from the projects I was on and wisdom given to me by my mentor throughout the term.
