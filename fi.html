Reducing friction in the credit card application process

Financial institution, 6 weeks

Context

Working with one of the top five banks in Canada, we were tasked with re-imagining the credit card application experience within six weeks. With millions of customers and the desire to be recognized in the market as a "digital-first brand", the current experience did not conform to best practices in web and mobile design nor the latest branding guidelines represented in the homepage. In addition, compared to its competitors, the application contained the highest number of required fields, leading to high friction in getting a prospective customer off the market.

Role

The team consisted of an interaction designer, a design lead, and myself (visual designer). I worked closely with the interaction designer to champion all aspects of the design process, from gathering and synthesizing research, to creating user flows and wireframes.

Process
