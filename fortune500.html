Request Handling and Analytics

Fortune 500 company

Context

Over the course of 8 weeks, I worked with a Fortune 500 transportation company to redefine how clerks do their job and increase visibility into the business' day-to-day operations and customers. Currently, requests and all subsequent data are processed and stored using an extremely complex and intricate system––tens of thousands of requests are submitted on a daily basis. We learned, from stakeholder interviews, of the inefficiencies that make it challenging to resolve requests in a timely manner and the lack of metrics on both a micro and macro level, hindering the success of the business.

Role

I worked closely with an interaction design lead to define the problem/scope, ideate and iterate on concepts and wireframes, execute on visual design, and establish a design system that would lay the foundation for subsequent stages of the project. Other members of the team included the project lead, technical architecture lead, business analyst, and testing lead.

Process
