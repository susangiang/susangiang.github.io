IBM Real Time Insights

Real-time data analytics tools for IoT devices

role

designer, internship, may - sep 2015

During my internship with IBM, I worked in a team of 4 UX Designers and 4 Software Developers on IBM Real Time Insights (IBM RTI), a real-time data analytics tool. For this project, I worked with another UX Designer to design the Rules Editor and partook in design walkthroughs (a playback in IBM design terms) as we iterated through the proposed designs.

the problem

finding problem areas fast and taking immediate action

It is the time of the Internet of Things (IoT) and it is growing exponentially. It is predicted that by 2020, the number of Internet-connected things will hit or surpass 50 billion. A concept that was foreign only a decade ago is now being adopted by all kinds of industries around the world. We found that manufacturers, who rely on the heavy usage of IoT devices attached to their machinery, simply cannot manage the high volume of devices to sustain an efficient manufacturing environment. How can we allow organizations to monitor equipment and operations to understand and respond to emerging conditions to improve responsiveness, equipment availability, and overall efficiency?

the solution

real time insights at the tip of your fingers

After two months of iterating with the designers, a complete web experience of the IBM RTI was designed and implemented. The IBM RTI can be used to allow organizations to monitor equipment and leverages the data collected from such devices to understand and respond to emerging conditions for increased responsiveness and efficiency.

Organizations can:

  • Connect and monitor devices to understand current conditions and status, usage patterns, availability, and performance
  • Monitor environmental conditions to identify security issues and unsafe or undesirable situations
  • Use the Rule Editor to automate a response to the conditions listed above
